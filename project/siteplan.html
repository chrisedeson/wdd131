Website Plan: NaijaTaste

Site Name

NaijaTaste

This name represents a website dedicated to showcasing the rich and diverse flavors of Nigerian cuisine.

Site Purpose

NaijaTaste aims to be an online resource dedicated to Nigerian cuisine. The site will feature traditional recipes, cooking tutorials, profiles of local chefs, and more. It will also offer a weekly newsletter and exclusive content for subscribers.

Scenarios

Questions that visitors might ask:

  • What are the best traditional Nigerian recipes?
  • How can I cook Nigerian dishes at home?

Color Schema

Selected Colors:

Primary Color (Green): Used for headings, links, and buttons.

Secondary Color (White): Used for background and paragraphs.

Accent Colors (Vibrant Patterns): Used for accents and highlights.

Typography

Selected Fonts:

  • Sansita Swashed for headings
  • Roboto Condensed for body text
